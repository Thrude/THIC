About Us

Built by Students,
for Students

THIC is organized by the ICS Investment Club — a student-led organization dedicated to bridging the gap between classroom theory and real-world finance. We believe every ambitious student deserves a platform to prove themselves.

Since our first edition, we have grown into Thailand's most competitive high school investment competition, attracting participants from leading international and bilingual schools across the country.
